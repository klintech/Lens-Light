About Me

The story behind the lens

Hello, I'm Alex Morgan

I'm a professional photographer based in New York with over 10 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments.

My journey with photography began when I was 16 years old, when my grandfather gave me my first camera. What started as a hobby quickly developed into a passion, and eventually, a fulfilling career.

I specialize in wedding photography, portraits, and event coverage, bringing a unique perspective and artistic vision to every photoshoot. My style combines classic techniques with contemporary approaches, resulting in timeless images that tell your story.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me hiking in nature, exploring new coffee shops, or spending time with my family and our golden retriever, Luna.

My Philosophy

Passion

Photography isn't just my job—it's my calling. I bring enthusiasm and dedication to every project, treating each client's story with the care it deserves.

Vision

I see beauty in the details others might miss. My goal is to capture not just how things look, but how they feel—the emotions, connections, and fleeting moments.

Connection

Building rapport with my clients is essential. When you feel comfortable and trust me, that's when the most authentic and stunning images emerge.
