Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

For weddings, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance to secure your date, especially during peak season (May-October). For portraits and other sessions, 2-4 weeks notice is usually sufficient, but availability may vary.

What is your payment policy?

+

A 30% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking, with the remaining balance due two weeks before the event date. For portrait sessions, full payment is due at the time of booking.

How many photos will I receive?

+

The number of photos varies depending on the package and event duration. Typically, weddings receive 500-800 edited images, portrait sessions 30-50 images, and events approximately 75-100 images per hour of coverage.

How long until I receive my photos?

+

Wedding photos are typically delivered within 4-6 weeks. Portrait sessions and events are delivered within 2-3 weeks. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you travel for photoshoots?

+

Yes! I'm available for travel within the United States and internationally. Travel fees apply for locations beyond 50 miles from New York City. Custom quotes are provided for destination weddings and events.
